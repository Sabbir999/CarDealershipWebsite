About Us

Welcome to Detroit Motors, where we provide the best car buying experience. We are proud to offer a wide range of vehicles, from luxury to economy, and we are committed to helping our customers find the perfect car that fits their needs.

Detroit Motors was established in 2020, and we have grown to become one of the most trusted car dealerships in the United States. We pride ourselves on providing exceptional customer service, a vast selection of quality vehicles, and competitive pricing that sets us apart from the competition

At Detroit Motors, we offer a diverse selection of vehicles to suit all lifestyles and budgets. Our inventory includes popular brands such as Ford, Chevrolet, and Honda, with a range of features that will meet your driving needs. Whether you are looking for a reliable family car, a fuel-efficient commuter vehicle, or a luxury sedan, we have the perfect car for you. Additionally, we offer special deals and financing options to help you get the best value for your money

Our team at Detroit Motors is made up of highly qualified professionals who are passionate about providing exceptional customer service. Our sales associates are knowledgeable about all the vehicles we offer and are committed to helping you find the perfect car. Our financing experts will work with you to find the best financing option for your budget, and our service technicians are trained to keep your car running smoothly for years to come.

Thank you for visiting Detroit Motors. We are located in six cities across Michigan, USA. We invite you to visit us at one of our six locations or give us a call to learn more about our inventory, financing options, and exceptional customer service
